My research is primarily focused on the development of AI tools for communities, specifically exploring the implementation of LLM agents with model-level interactions or user-centered evaluations. I aim to create tools that embrace social consciousness, beyond considerations of model precision and performance.

I hold a BS in Information Science at Cornell University. I am an incoming Computer Science MS at Stanford University.

Currently, I am conducting research on LLM Agent development and Perceived Harm research at KIXLAB at Kaist University and at the Stanford ESRG Lab.

Seunghee Lee


My work focuses on areas of HCI with main interests in Autnomous System Interaction, Information Visualization, and Tangible Technology Research. Applying design and engineering, I hope to empower users and present a novel approach to solving social issues. I am interested in areas of Autnomous Vehicle Interaction, Sustainable Technology, and inventing seamless Visual & Haptic Technology for enchancing human experiences with interaction with complex systems. Currently I am an undergrad studying Information Science (B.S.) with a concentration in Data Science and Interactive Technology at Cornell University. I am currently doing Human Robot Interaction Telepresence research in the Makeability Lab at Cornell.

This summer(2021) I am worked at the KIXlab designing an In-vehicle Creative Media Experience along with Hyundai Motors. Also, I am working at the Creative Computing Group at Sogang University working on Eternal Testimony, an interactive hologram interview of the Comfort women. Additionally, I am doing research at the Social Media Group at Cornell, The AI Media Lab at Sogang University, and the Visual Media Group at KAIST University.

I have previously worked with Cochl. and Dooboolab as a Software Engineer on projects like Mercedez Benz Sound AI. At school, I'm part of Cornell Data Science Insights Team and participated in projects like MyCourseIndex.com and Coronavirus Twitter Analysis.

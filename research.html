Research

My research interest is in developing systems for Human Collaboration with AI. In the past, I’ve worked in domains ranging from content moderation, Autonomous Behicles, to Sound Recognition AI. Through my research, I am interested in developing interactive methods for Human AI Collaboration for communities that are effected by AI. I'm interested in research opportunities that look at domain specific uses of AI.


Research Interests : Human AI Interaction, Domain Specific XAI Applications, Model Interpertability & Robustness

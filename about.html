About




My work examines human experiences with relation to spatial and visual elements in technology. Applying design and engineering, I hope to enhance human experiences of processing information through the help of spatial and visual elements. Currently I am an undergrad studying Information Science (B.S.) with a concentration in Data Science and Interactive Technology at Cornell University.

This summer(2021) I am working at the KIXlab designing an In-vehicle Creative Media Experience along with Hyundai Motors. Also, I am working at the Creative Computing Group at Sogang University working on Eternal Testimony, an interactive hologram interview of the Comfort women. Additionally, I am doing research at the Social Media Group at Cornell, The AI Media Lab at Sogang University, and the Visual Media Group at KAIST University.

I have previously worked with Cochl. and Dooboolab as a Software Engineer on projects like Mercedez Benz Sound AI. At school, I'm part of Cornell Data Science Insights Team and participated in projects like MyCourseIndex.com and Coronavirus Twitter Analysis.
